## 5.5 Expandable Direction

The following table lists the directions in which an expandable ad may expand, given the positioning of the ad unit on the page and constraints imposed by the content.

| **Value** | **Description** |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Left |
| 2 | Right |
| 3 | Up |
| 4 | Down |
| 5 | Full Screen |
